Study Buddy Features

⏰

Study Timer

Set focused study sessions with our built-in timer. Choose from 5, 10, 15, 20, or 30-minute sessions to match your attention span.

🔥

Streak Tracking

Build momentum by studying every day! Watch your streak grow and feel motivated to keep the fire burning.

🏆

Achievement Badges

Unlock special badges as you hit milestones: Getting Started (3 days), Week Warrior (7 days), Study Champion (30 days), and more!

📈

Progress Dashboard

See your study stats, subject breakdown, and calendar view of your study history. Track your improvement over time!
